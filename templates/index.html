Marine Invertebrates

Crustaceans are a diverse group of mostly aquatic invertebrate animals within the arthropods, traditionally classified under the subphylum Crustacea. They include crabs, lobsters, shrimp, barnacles, and many others. Crustaceans are now considered part of a larger group called Pancrustacea, which also includes insects, showing their close evolutionary relationship.
